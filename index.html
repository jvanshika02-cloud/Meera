Auspicious Avoid

General Yatra Guidelines

🌙Avoid Amavasya (new moon): Not recommended for starting new journeys — focus on family rituals instead.
⚡Avoid Rahu Kaal: Each day has a 90-minute inauspicious window. Never start your journey during this time.
✨Ekadashi & Purnima are excellent: Starting a yatra on these tithis is considered highly auspicious across traditions.
🌅Brahma Muhurat (4–6 AM): The most sacred hour for prayer and departure — if possible, begin travel in the early morning.
📿Wednesday & Friday: Generally auspicious for pilgrimage, especially for Devi temples and shrines.
